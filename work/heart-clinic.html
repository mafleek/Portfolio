HEART Clinic Documentation System

A browser-based workflow that unifies live physiological tracking, structured session data, and AI-assisted SOAP documentation, with faculty oversight built in.

View Prototype Download Final Report (PDF) User Flows (PDF)
Role
UX Research • UX Design • Interaction Design • Responsible AI framing
Focus
Clinical workflow clarity, cognitive load reduction, transparency of AI assistance, and safe supervisor review patterns.
Why it matters
Student clinicians were stitching together Polar data, paper templates, Excel calculators, and Word docs. The system turns that chaos into a single guided flow, without turning AI into a black box.

Key screens

What I shipped (as a prototype)

  • Guided stepper flow: device connection → session setup → live session → review → note generation → submission.
  • AI assistance that’s visibly distinct from user-entered content, so “help” doesn’t masquerade as truth.
  • Faculty review experience designed for speed, clarity, and revision requests.
  • System status visibility (recording, syncing, autosave) to reduce uncertainty during live care.
